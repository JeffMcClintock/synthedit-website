— How it works

Drag. Connect. Ship.

A complete environment for synthesizer design — from the first sine wave to a finished VST3 plugin in your DAW.

Modular Canvas · my-first-synth.se1 Playing
— 01 / Visual Patching

Build synths by connecting modules.

Drag oscillators, filters, envelopes and effects onto the canvas. Connect them with virtual patch cables. Hear the results instantly. No programming knowledge needed — just curiosity.

Learn the basics →
Panel Designer · phasm.gui Editing
— 02 / Professional Output

Export as VST3 plugins.

Turn your patches into standalone VST3 plugins. Design custom interfaces with knobs, sliders, switches and bitmap graphics. Your plugins work in every major DAW on Windows and macOS.
