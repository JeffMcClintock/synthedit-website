SynthEdit has an active community of plugin builders, module developers, and synth enthusiasts spanning forums, chat rooms, wikis, and tutorial sites. The links below are a good place to ask questions, share patches, and learn from people who have been building with SynthEdit for years.

## Forums & Chat

- **[SynthEdit@groups.io](https://groups.io/g/synthedit)** — the main discussion group for support, collaboration, and bug reports.
- **[SynthEdit Discord](https://discord.gg/eErPWDZnsC)** — real-time chat about plugin building.
- **[SynthEdit on Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/groups/129301030415489/)** — Facebook group for SynthEdit users.
- **[SynthEdit Forum](http://syntheditforum.boards.net/)** — message board.
- **[KVR Modular Synthesis forum](http://www.kvraudio.com/forum/viewforum.php?f=23)** — general modular synth discussion on KVR Audio.
- **[SynthEdit i Sverige](http://synthedit.proboards.com/index.cgi)** — Swedish-language SynthEdit forum.
- **[nay-seven](http://nay-seven.com/index.php)** — French-language SynthEdit forum.
- **[SynthEdit @ DSP Central](https://github.com/resonancedesign/synthedit.dspcentral.info)** — repository of SynthEdit creations.
- **[syntheditusers.org](https://syntheditusers.org/)** — files and prefabs preserved from the defunct Yahoo Group.

## Tutorials & Online Resources

- **[SynthEdit Wiki](https://syntheditwiki.miraheze.org/wiki/1.4:Main_Page)** — community-maintained wiki.
- **[SynthEdit help and tutorials](https://synthedit-help.co.uk/)** — tutorials and guides.
- **[SynthEdit Tutorials by Cajhmere](https://syntheditstepbystep.wordpress.com/)** — step-by-step tutorial blog.
- **[Quick-Start Guide at BIPTUNIA](https://biptunia.com/?p=2637)** — beginner walkthrough.
- **[Tutorials by JWP](https://www.patreon.com/jwppatreon)** — video tutorials on Patreon.
- **[SEHub](https://psytranceplugins.com/syedit.htm)** — utility for browsing and sharing SynthEdit resources.

## Books

- **[Visual VST/i-Programming](http://www.wizoobooks.com/)** — e-book by Schoffhauzer, Haupt & Fortune covering plugin development with SynthEdit.
- **[Welsh's Synthesizer Cookbook](http://amzn.com/B000ERHA4S)** — recipes for recreating classic synth sounds.
- **[Becoming a Synthesizer Wizard](https://www.amazon.ca/Becoming-Synthesizer-Wizard-Presets-Power/dp/1598635506)** — guide to designing presets and understanding synthesis.
