## Why SynthEdit?

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Visual Modular Design" icon="pencil">
    Build synthesizers by connecting modules together visually. No coding required — just drag, drop, and connect.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Create VST Plugins" icon="setting">
    Export your creations as VST3 plugins that work in any major DAW — Cubase, Ableton Live, FL Studio, and more.
  </Card>
  <Card title="No Licensing Restrictions" icon="approve-check-circle">
    You own what you create. Sell your plugins commercially with no royalties or licensing fees.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Learn Synthesis" icon="open-book">
    Perfect for learning how synthesizers work. See the signal flow, experiment with modules, and hear results instantly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Huge Module Library" icon="list-format">
    Hundreds of built-in modules — oscillators, filters, envelopes, effects, MIDI processors, and more.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Third-Party Modules" icon="add-document">
    Extend SynthEdit with community-created modules, or build your own using the C++ SDK.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Used by Plugin Developers Worldwide

SynthEdit has been used to create thousands of commercial and free VST plugins, including award-winning instruments and effects used in professional music production.
