---
title: Voice Splitter
description: Splits a polyphonic signal into individual voice outputs.
---

The **Voice Splitter** separates a polyphonic signal into individual voice outputs. This is primarily a diagnostic tool for analyzing individual voices in a polyphonic patch.

## Usage

Connect the Voice Splitter to a polyphonic signal to access each voice separately. Useful for:
- Recording individual voices to separate audio files
- Debugging polyphony issues by monitoring specific voices
- Creating per-voice effects processing
