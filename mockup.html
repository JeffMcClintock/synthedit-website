Hi — I'm Jeff.

I started writing SynthEdit in 1995, on a Pentium running Windows 95. The idea was simple: make synthesis accessible to anyone with a computer and an ear, without needing to learn DSP or C++ first.

Three decades later, thousands of commercial plugins have been built with it, and a generation of audio developers cut their teeth here. SynthEdit is still independently developed — no investors, no royalties, no telemetry.

— Jeff McClintock
